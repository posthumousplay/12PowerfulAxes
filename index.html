What are the 12 Powerful Axes Values?

There are 12 independent axes, where each has two opposing values assigned to them. They are:

South vs North
South favour personal metaphysics, glowie-type relations, civilization flexibility, interpersonal reactivity. North favour impersonal metaphysics, tukame-type relations, civilization holism, interpersonal permission.

Personal Metaphysics vs Impersonal Metaphysics
Personal Metaphysics favour beliefs like self, free will, strong particular friendship, human over animal, higher scores are metaphysical pluralism, human privilige and anti-north sentiment. Impersonal Metaphysics favor non-self theory, determinism, equality for animals, universal unity, higher scores are absolute monism, conjoined personality, anti-south sentiment, animal privilige, extreme spirituality.

Glowie-Type Relations vs Tukame-Type Relations
Glowie-Type Relations favor punish non-loyal people, sexual liberty, friendly relations, loyal society without things like school bullying, higher scores are brutal school discipline, fanatical friendship, perfect and skilled society fundamentalism. Tukame-Type Relations favor absolute free speech, tolerate toxic people, monitoring sexual security, school liberalism, non-attachment, higher scores are tolerance of toxic relations, abolitionism of punishment, pathological detachment and blaming the lawmakers.

Civilization Flexibility vs Civilization Holism
Civilization Flexibility favor tolerating different paths of development of civilization, multiple understanding of social and technological progress, higher scores are extreme flexibility and seeing unrealistic, utopian plans and contextuality can consider systems generally considered pure evil. Civilization Holism favor is that certain principles of civilization, progress, freedom are universal, these people are inclined to believe, for example, that equality is always social progress, higher scores are blocking other avenues of civilization, trying to create a new world order what will block the leakage of flexibility, terror against southern activists.

Interpersonal Reactivity vs Interpersonal Permission
Interpersonal reactivity favor punish bad people and criminals, beliefs tolerance barrier, culture power, spiritual doctrine, higher scores are fanaticism, punish other beliefs, structural culture. interpersonal permission favor tolerance of other behaviour and beliefs, spiritual and cultural tolerance, rehabilitation criminals, higher scores are permissivism, absolute tolerance for rotten behaviour, spiritual blurring and extreme open multiculturalism, extreme forgiveness, toleration of violence.

Left vs Right
Left favour economical control, collectivism, reformation, tech & progress, libertianism, ecology & other left. Right favour economical liberty, individualism, conservation, mammoth & regress, authoritarianism, disecology & other right.

Economical Control vs Economical Liberty
Economical control favor protectionism, regulations, higher taxes, higher scores are central-planned economy and extreme high taxes. Economical Liberty favor deregulation, low taxes, free trade, higher scores are economical darwinism, no taxes, laissez-faire.

Collectivism vs Individualism
Collectivism favor social empathy, collective property, collective over person, public schools and some other branches, higher scores are hiperempathy, collective zerg-like consciousness, full collective economy. Individualism favor person over collective, private property, higher score are childish egocentrism, social atomism and avaritionism.

Reformation vs Conservation
Reformation favor open-minded society, tolerance, democracy, equality and belief in social change, higher scores favor multiculturalism, extreme social equality and liberty, pacifism. Conservation favor "natural world order", monarchism and belief in conservatism, higher scores are reactionism, militarism and dogmatic order.

Tech & Progress vs Mammoth & Regress
Tech & Progress favor technology, technological body enchancement, programming reality, genetical engineery, like to make in future superintelligent AI, higher scores are absolute virtuality, technological cyborgs, posthumanism etc. Mammoth & Regress favor unity with nature, not like smartphones, technical regress, higher scores are primitivism, prehistorical regress and technophobia.

Libertarianism vs Authoritarianism
Libertarianism favor liberty, lower intervention of state to civic houses, drug use legalisation, higher scores are state abolitionism and anarchy. Authoritarianism favor civic control, civic rights violation, higher security, higher scores are totalitarianism and political violence.

Ecology & Other Left vs Disecology & Other Right
Ecology & Other Left favor ecological regulations, immigration liberty, secularism, state centralization, higher scores are ethnic abolitionism, state atheism etc. Disecology & Other Right favor ecology denialism, immigration control, decentrelization, religious state, higher scores are anti-immigration sentiment, religious fundamentalism etc.

Rotation vs Standard
Rotation favor alternative values and violation. Standard favor classic values and academicism.

Alternative Values vs Classic Values
Alternative Values favor higher level of acceptation and power of alternative values. Classic Values favor higher level of acceptation earth classic values.

Violation vs Academicism
Higher level of Violation mean violent relations, controversial beliefs, higher scores mean genocide, sometimes outlaw nature. Higher level of Academicism favor academic debate and mainstream beliefs, higher scores mean non-violent academicism.
